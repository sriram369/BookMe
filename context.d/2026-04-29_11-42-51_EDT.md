## Payment Decision

Current MVP:

- No live payment collection.
- Payment is represented as `Pay at property`.

Production direction:

- Razorpay should be the default India payment provider.
- Payment links should be generated server-side.
- Payment status must update through webhooks.
- The LLM must not decide refunds, discounts, or paid status.
- Alternatives to keep possible:
  - Cashfree
  - Stripe
  - Google Pay/UPI through a supported provider
